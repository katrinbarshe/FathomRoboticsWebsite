Operational Capabilities
FathomOps Operations and Maintenance for Maritime Autonomous Systems

FathomOps provides the people, processes, and standards that sustain mission-ready performance across maritime autonomous fleets. Our mariners, USV pilots, and maintenance teams follow standardized procedures that ensure safe, reliable, and environmentally responsible operations for multi-vendor uncrewed surface vessels.

Built on internationally recognized management standards for quality, safety, and environmental compliance, FathomOps delivers turnkey operations, maintenance, and piloting support to keep every vessel mission-ready.

FathomRed Threat Replication and Opposition Force Services

FathomRed delivers realistic, repeatable threat replication to prepare warfighters for the challenges of emerging autonomous systems.

Using modified uncrewed surface and ground vessels (USVs and UGVs), our teams emulate adversary signatures, profiles, and behaviors for live-fire, electronic warfare, and weapons test and evaluation (WT&E) events.

Each platform is configured to replicate specific threat dynamics, while Fathom operators manage deployment, control, and data capture throughout the mission. FathomRed enables Fleet and range customers to conduct complex, high-fidelity exercises that validate tactics and strengthen readiness across training and test environments.

FathomX Training and Education for Expeditionary UxS

FathomX teaches servicemembers how to build, integrate, and operate uncrewed systems from the ground up.

Through hands-on instruction, participants learn the core components of autonomy including power, propulsion, sensors, networks, and software, and how to assemble and configure them into fully functional surface, ground, or aerial systems.

Built around a train-the-trainer approach, FathomX prepares Navy and Special Operations personnel to teach and sustain autonomy within their own units. Courses use COTS-based hardware and software adaptable across multiple vessel types and are conducted in expeditionary environments that reflect real operational conditions.
